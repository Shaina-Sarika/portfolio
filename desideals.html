DesiDeals

E-Commerce website

Project Overview

The E-commerce website project aims to create an online shopping platform that enables customers to purchase a wide range of items from various categories, such as fashion, electronics, books, and more. The website will have a user-friendly interface that allows customers to browse through the available items, select their preferred items, and add them to their shopping cart. To access the website's features, customers will need to create an account and log in using their login credentials. Once logged in, customers can add items to the wishlist, save their payment and shipping details and view their order history. The website will have a comprehensive search feature that enables customers to search for products by category, keyword or brand. The product listings will include product images, descriptions, prices, ratings and customer reviews to help customers make informed decisions. The shopping cart feature will allow customers to add multiple items to their cart, view the total cost of their purchase, and proceed to checkout. Overall, the e-commerce website project aims to provide a seamless and enjoyable online shopping experience for customers while providing a reliable platform for sellers to showcase their products and reach a wider audience.

Tech Stack

Front-end: HTML, CSS, Javascript, Bootstrap, jQuery
Back-end: PHP
Other tools and technologies: Git (for version control)
